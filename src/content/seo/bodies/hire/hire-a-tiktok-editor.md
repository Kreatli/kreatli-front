## What is a TikTok content editor?

A TikTok editor turns your raw footage — UGC, podcast video, product demos, lifestyle B-roll, stream highlights — into platform-native short videos. TikTok-specialist editors know:

- **Hook density** — visual or verbal payoff in the first 1–2 seconds, not 5
- **Trend literacy** — when to use trending sounds, text overlays, and stitch/duet-friendly framing (without copying copyrighted audio you cannot license)
- **Caption culture** — on-screen text that works muted; emoji and line breaks that match TikTok norms
- **Loop-friendly endings** — cuts that encourage rewatches
- **Batch variation** — same source, multiple angles/hooks for A/B posting

This role differs from a "TikTok manager" who might also handle strategy and posting. On Kreatli Marketplace, you are hiring **editing labour** — clarify in your brief if you need upload/scheduling too.

## FAQ

### Do I need a TikTok editor if I already cross-post YouTube Shorts?

Shorts and TikTok share vertical format but **different cultures**. TikTok editors often punch harder on hooks, on-screen text, and cut speed. If Shorts cross-posts underperform on TikTok, a TikTok-native editor usually fixes pacing and caption style — not just re-exporting the same file.

### Can editors use trending sounds on my brand account?

Trending audio can boost reach but carries **licensing and regional restrictions** for business accounts. Specify in your brief: approved sound sources, no unlicensed tracks, or "editor suggests, I approve before post." Responsible editors flag this upfront.

### Should I hire one TikTok editor or a small team?

Solo creators and small brands usually start with **one dedicated editor** who learns your voice. Agencies scale with multiple editors only after SOPs and style guides exist. Kreatli Marketplace fits both — post one job for a trial, then expand scope.

No. Content-rewards clipping pays per views on **clipper-owned accounts**. Kreatli Marketplace is **fixed-price editor hire** for clips on **your** TikTok.
