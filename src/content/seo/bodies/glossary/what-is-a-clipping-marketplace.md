# What Is a Clipping Marketplace?

A **clipping marketplace** is an online platform that connects people who need short-form video clips with people who produce them. The term covers two very different business models — and picking the wrong one is one of the most common mistakes creators and editors make.

Some marketplaces pay clippers **per view** through content-rewards campaigns. Others, like **Kreatli Marketplace**, are **video editor marketplaces** where creators hire freelance clippers for fixed project pay.

## Why it matters for creators and clippers

"Clipping marketplace" sounds specific, but the mechanics differ completely:

| Model | Who gets paid | How pay is calculated | Typical deliverable |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Content-rewards marketplace | Clippers who post | Views / CPM / campaign rules | Clips on poster accounts |
| Editor marketplace (Kreatli) | Hired freelance editors | Fixed price per job | Clips to creator's channel |

Creators who want a reliable Shorts pipeline need the second model. Editors who want predictable freelance income need it too. Content-rewards marketplaces solve distribution, not production hiring.

## Examples

**Content-rewards clipping marketplace:** A fitness brand funds a $2,000 reward pool. Clippers join the campaign, edit workout highlights, post from their own Instagram accounts, and earn based on view performance. The brand optimises for reach across many posters.

**Video editor marketplace:** A business coach posts a job on Kreatli Marketplace: "10 clips per week from my podcast, hook-first style, burned-in captions, deliver by Friday." A vetted clip editor is hired at $35 per clip. Finished files land on the coach's TikTok and LinkedIn — no view lottery involved.

Same category name, different job to be done.

## FAQ

### Is Fiverr a clipping marketplace?

Fiverr is a **general freelance marketplace** with video editing gigs, including some clipping packages. It is not clipping-specialist — quality ranges widely, and the gig model suits one-off orders more than weekly pipelines. Kreatli Marketplace is built specifically for creator ↔ clipper hiring workflows.

### Can one marketplace do both models?

Some ecosystems offer multiple products, but the **payment mechanics** stay distinct. Content rewards pay for distribution performance. Editor marketplaces pay for editing labour. Mixing the terms causes confusion — always check whether you are hiring an editor or funding a view-based campaign.

### How do I hire a clipper on an editor marketplace?

Post a job with clear deliverables: source content type (podcast, stream, course), clip count, aspect ratio (usually 9:16), caption style, turnaround, and budget. Review editor profiles, agree price, and receive finished clips. On Kreatli Marketplace, start at [/jobs/create](/jobs/create).
